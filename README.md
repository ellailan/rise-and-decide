### *Because life is hard, but getting out of bed is harder.*
A predictive analytics tool that finally solves this crisis for UBC students.

Traditional decision-making is slow, unreliable, and emotionally biased.  
We knew there had to be a better way…

## 🤖 Our Solution  
We built the most over-engineered bed-leaving algorithm in human history. Works on mobile and desktop. 

**Rise & Decide** calculates a **morning importance score** using:

- ☁️ **Live Vancouver weather, pulled from an API**
- 😭 **A Mood slider**
- 🎓 **Course difficulty ranking**  
- 💕 **Crush-in-class multiplier**  
  (+200 points for the thrill of locking eyes)
- ⏰ **Time of day logic**  
  (“If it’s afternoon you already missed it”)
